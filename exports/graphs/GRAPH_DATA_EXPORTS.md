## Chart 3: SEO vs GEO Distribution Curves (Slide 8)

**Type**: Dual distribution curves comparison

### Traditional Google SEO (Linear Distribution)
```
Traffic
  │
  │\
  │ \
  │  \
  │   \
  │    \_______  (long tail)
  └──────────────── Ranking Position
       1  5  10  15  20+
```
- Score range: 30–90 points
- Progressive, gradual decline
- Long tail survives

### Gemini GEO (Cliff-Edge/Binary Opposition)
```
Exposure
  │
  │████
  │████
  │████
  │          (cliff drop)
  └──────────────── Citation Spots
       1  2  3  4  5
```
- Only 5-10 citation spots ("Golden Seats")
- Win or lose — binary outcome
- Top 5% authority or invisible
