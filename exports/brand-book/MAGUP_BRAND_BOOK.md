## Component Library

### Cards

**Standard Card**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` with subtle shadow
- Border: 1px `#E8E0F5` or none
- Border radius: 12–16px
- Padding: 24px
- Shadow: `0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`

**Purple Tinted Card**
- Background: `#F0EAFF` / `#EDE5FF`
- Border: 1px `#D4C4FF`
- Border radius: 12–16px
- Used for: info callouts, feature highlights

**Dark Purple Card**
- Background: `#3D1A6E` / `#5B2D8E`
- Text: `#FFFFFF`
- Used for: category badges at top of columns

## Logo & Brand Mark

### MagUp Logo
- **Text mark**: "MagUp" — mixed case, clean sans-serif
- **Icon**: Appears to use an upward arrow motif combined with "M" letterform
- **Variants observed**:
  - Dark text on light background (primary)
  - Light/white text on dark purple background
  - Small icon-only variant with stylized "M" + arrow

### Arrow Motif
- Recurring 3D purple gradient arrow pointing upward-right
- Used as hero visual element (slides 1 and 24)
- Gradient: purple → pink/coral transition
- Semi-transparent, glossy 3D rendering

## Brand Voice & Messaging

### Tone
- **Professional but accessible** — Not overly technical
- **Benefit-first** — Lead with outcomes, not features
- **Data-driven** — Use concrete numbers and percentages
- **Global mindset** — Cross-cultural, multilingual emphasis

- "Content Marketing Brain"
- "GEO + SEO"
- "1000+ Global Enterprises"
- "Not Only Traffic, But Also Conversion"
